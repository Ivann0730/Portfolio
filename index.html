About me

I'm Ivann James Paradero, a 21-year-old Computer Science student at Cebu Institute of Technology – University. My work spans from low-level systems to user-facing experiences — Java RPG engines, Android apps in Kotlin, and a PHP parking management system.

I thrive at the intersection of problem-solving and creativity. Whether I'm optimizing a query or designing a flow that works for real users, I'm motivated by the craft of making things that actually work well.
